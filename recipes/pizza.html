Pizza

Description

A baked dish originating in Italy, which consists of a flat, round, crispy leavened dough covered with a layer of sauce and a layer of one or more toppings, such as meat, seafood, vegetables, and fruit, which is often topped with a layer of cheese. A popular dish served for all types of occassions and meals, Pizza is made as a sweet or a savory food that can be enjoyed as a breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, or snack.

Traditionally, Pizza is often considered to be a savory food made into a round shape and cut into wedges for serving. However, as foods change, Pizza has evolved to be formed into round, rectangular and square shapes with a wide variety of ingredients being used to make the toppings for the baked crust. Sweet pizzas are often served for breakfast, brunch, lunch, dessert, or as an appetizer while the savory varieties are most often served for lunch, dinner, or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of floor (divided on half).
  • 1 spoon of yeast.
  • 1 spoon of sugar.
  • 2/3 cups of warm water + 2 spoons of warm water.
  • 2 sprays of oil on antihaderent on non-stick spray.
  • 1 cup of marinara sauce.
  • 6 ounces of shredded mozzarela cheese.

Steps

  1. Wash your hands whith water and soup.
  2. Pre-heat the oven at 375 grades F.
  3. Gather, crush and measure all the ingredients before start the recipe.
  4. On a medium bowl, mix 1 cup of floor, yeast, sugar and water.
  5. Gradually add the other cup of floor until the mix become a ball
  6. Knead the mass for 5 minutes
  7. Spray a bowl whit a anti-stick oil spray. Put the mass on the bowl and cover it whit a clean cloth. Put the bowl into the oven while it is pre-heating and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  8. Spray a 12x16 inches tray whith non-stick oil spray. Extend the mass as a uniform layer on the tray
  9. Add a thin layer of marinara sauce , leaving 1/2 inch of crust on all edges
  10. Add shredded cheese and toppings. Be careful to not put to many ingredientsor the pizza will come out wet
  11. Bake it for 15 minutes until the edges become golden, the chesse melt and it be ligthly golden on some parts
  12. Refrigerate the leftovers within 2 hous after finish the recipe. Eat the pizza whitin 3 or 4 days
